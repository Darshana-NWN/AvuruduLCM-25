Avurudu Nakath Times 2025

Auspicious times for the Sinhala & Tamil New Year (Example Dates - Update with Official 2025 Times)

Punya Kalaya

April 13, Evening - April 14, Morning

The transitional period (non-auspicious time) for religious observances.

Aluth Avurudu Udawa

April 14, Morning

Sinhala & Tamil New Year dawns.

Lip Ginni Meleweema

April 14, Morning

Lighting the hearth to prepare the first meal.

Weda Alleema & Ganu Denu

April 14, Morning

Commencing work and performing the first transaction.

Ahara Anubhawaya

April 14, Morning

Partaking of the first meal of the New Year.

Hisa Thel Gama

April 16 or later, Morning

Anointing oil on the head for health and blessings.
