Event Day Agenda

9:00 AM

Arrival & Registration

Welcome registration of participants.

9:30 - 10:00 AM

Opening Ceremony

Traditional Kiri Ithirima & Oil Lamp Lighting.

10:00 - 10:20 AM

Welcome Speech

Welcome address by the Local Committee President.

10:20 - 10:30 AM

Appreciation Ceremony

Recognition of Members of the Month.

10:30 - 10:45 AM

Jives

Get energized with a fun dance session!

10:45 - 11:30 AM

Avurudu Kama Mesaya

Indulge in a traditional New Year feast.

11:30 AM - 12:50 PM

Avurudu Games - Part 1

Exciting traditional games session.

12:50 PM - 2:40 PM

Avurudu Games - Part 2

More games including Tug of War & Avurudu Kumaraya/Kumariya.

2:40 PM - 3:00 PM

Fun Break & Activities

Engaging activities and short break.

3:00 - 3:30 PM

Pani Bambare

Traditional game signifying unity and teamwork.

3:30 - 4:30 PM

Closing & Awards

Avurudu Kumaraya/Kumariya crowning & prize distribution.

4:30 - 5:00 PM

Vote of Thanks & Final Jives

Closing remarks and final celebratory dances.
